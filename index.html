Your todos are locked tighter than your ex's DMs.

How to Use (Yes, Even You Can Do This)

Step-by-step instructions so basic, even your grandma could follow. No, really.

Sign up or continue as guest. Takes 30 seconds max.

Step 2: Add Your Chaos

Click + or Cmd+Enter. Type. Hit Enter. Done.

One-click filters (All/Pending/Complete). Drag-drop priorities. Themes in settings.

No internet? No problem. Everything works.

Tasks sync when back online. Install as PWA for app-like experience.
